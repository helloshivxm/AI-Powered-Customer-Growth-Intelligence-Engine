# AI Powered Customer Growth Intelligence Engine

## Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end **Customer Growth Intelligence Engine** designed to transform raw customer transaction data into actionable business insights. Built for top analytics consulting firms, it combines **data analytics, machine learning, and business intelligence** to answer critical questions about customer value, retention, and growth.

## Objectives
- Identify high-value customers
- Improve customer retention
- Optimize marketing investments
- Increase customer lifetime value
- Support data-driven decision making

## Tools & Technologies
- **Languages & Libraries:** Python, SQL, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, XGBoost  
- **Visualization & BI:** Power BI  
- **Version Control:** GitHub  
- **Machine Learning Models:** Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, Deep Neural Networks (DNN)

## Workflow
1. **Data Audit & Cleaning** – Ensured high-quality, reliable customer records  
2. **Feature Engineering** – Created RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) variables  
3. **Customer Segmentation** – Grouped customers into Champions, Loyal, At Risk, Lost  
4. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis** – Identified revenue concentration  
5. **Cohort Retention Analysis** – Measured churn and retention patterns over time  
6. **Predictive Modeling** – Classified customers into High, Medium, Low value groups  
7. **Business Recommendations** – Actionable strategies for retention & growth  

## Key Results
- **Random Forest Model** achieved **99.91% accuracy** in customer classification  
- Identified that **8% of customers (Champions)** generate **46.9% of revenue**  
- Revealed that **most churn occurs in the first 90 days** of customer lifecycle  
- Designed strategies for **retention, upselling, and win-back campaigns**

## Business Impact
