# Executive Summary

Customer data is one of the most valuable assets available to modern businesses. Every customer interaction generates information that can be used to understand purchasing behavior, improve customer retention, and increase revenue. However, most organizations struggle to transform large volumes of transaction data into meaningful business insights.

This project was developed to build an end-to-end Customer Growth Intelligence Engine capable of converting raw customer transaction records into actionable business intelligence.

The project follows a structured analytics workflow beginning with data auditing, data cleaning, feature engineering, customer segmentation, customer lifetime value analysis, cohort analysis, and predictive machine learning.

The primary goal was to answer key business questions:

- Which customers generate the highest value?
- Which customers should be prioritized for retention?
- How can customer engagement be improved?
- Which customer groups contribute most to revenue?
- How can data support business growth decisions?

To address these questions, customer purchasing patterns were analyzed and transformed into meaningful customer intelligence metrics. Multiple analytical techniques were applied to understand customer behavior from different business perspectives.

The project includes:

- Data Quality Assessment
- Data Cleaning and Preparation
- Feature Engineering
- RFM Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis
- Cohort Analysis
- Customer Engagement Analysis
- Machine Learning Model Development
- Business Recommendation Framework

Four machine learning algorithms were trained and evaluated:

1. Logistic Regression
2. Random Forest
3. XGBoost
4. Deep Neural Network (DNN)

After comparing all models, Random Forest delivered the highest overall performance with an accuracy of 99.91%, making it the final selected model for customer classification.

The final solution enables businesses to:

- Identify high-value customers
- Improve customer retention efforts
- Optimize marketing investments
- Increase customer lifetime value
- Support data-driven decision making
- Build scalable customer growth strategies

This project demonstrates how analytics and artificial intelligence can be combined to transform customer transaction data into a practical business intelligence system capable of generating measurable business value.
